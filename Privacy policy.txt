Privacy Policy for BlueKeys
Effective Date: May 29, 2026

Thank you for using BlueKeys.

BlueKeys is an Android app that turns your device into a Bluetooth keyboard and mouse. Your privacy is important to us. It works locally using Bluetooth, without needing the internet, servers, or cloud accounts.

1. No Data Collection or Sharing
- No Personal Info: BlueKeys does not collect, store, track, or share any personal information.
- No Accounts: You do not need to create an account, log in, or sync with any cloud service to use the app.
- Keystrokes & Inputs: The app sends your keystrokes and mouse movements directly to your connected device in real time. We never log, save, or send your inputs to anyone.

2.What we collect
- Feedback: If you choose to send us feedback( includes last 10 error and crash logs-optional), we collect it using the formspree endpoint (https://formspree.io). This is the only time the app sends any information online, and it is completely optional. 

3. Permissions We Need
To work properly, BlueKeys needs these permissions:
- Nearby Devices / Bluetooth: To find, pair, and connect with your computers or other devices.
- Foreground Service / Notifications: To keep the connection stable while you are using the app. This is used solely to maintain your active Bluetooth keyboard and mouse connection in the background so your inputs are not interrupted.
- Bluetooth System Settings: To turn Bluetooth on when you start the app.

These permissions are strictly used to make the app work. We do not use them for tracking.

4.NO Internet Usage
- Core features work fully offline.
- We do not use advertising SDKs, tracking tools, analytics, or payment processors.

5.only internet usages :
- only required to send feedback and download google fonts

6. Compatibility
- Works on Android 9 and newer.
- Connects to Windows, macOS, Linux, and Android devices.

7. Children's Privacy
- BlueKeys does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

8. Your Responsibility
- Only connect to devices you trust.
- Keep your paired devices secure.

9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
